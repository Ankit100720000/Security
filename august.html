युक्तः कर्मफलं त्यक्त्वा शान्तिमाप्नोति नैष्ठिकीम्। अयुक्तः कामकारेण फले सक्तो निबध्यते॥

Translation

The disciplined one, renouncing the fruits of action, attains steady peace.
The undisciplined, driven by desire and attached to results, becomes bound.
